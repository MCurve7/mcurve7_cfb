Penalties

The data source I am using is missing a penalty on Alabama according to two different sources. I have added a row to the table below (hence the weird penalty type) and fixed the graph.

Georgia was the more penalize team... at least the team that had more penalties called on them. As always the data doesn't record uncalled penalties. I'm not sure how many missed penalties there were in either direction. But overall they were the more penalized team and we were not able to capitalize on that. And that's all I got to say about that.
