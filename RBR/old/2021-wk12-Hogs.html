Newish graph

While thinking about our early season high SRs on 3rd as balloons has pointed out, I started thinking about what those numbers mean. One thing that occurred to me is that there are likely to be more 1st downs than 2nd and more 2nd downs than 3rd, hence the difference between the SR by Drive by Down calculated by Down and SR by Drive by Down calculated by Drive. (I really need to rename those!) Looking at the tables (added at the bottom of this post in case anyone wants to look at them) didn't help...

So I decided to add an annotation to an existing chart. I took the Offensive Drives by Drive and added the down for each play. Now looking at the SR by Drive by Down calculated by Down (reproduced below) we can see that on Drive 3 we are at a SR of 1.0 for both 2nd and 3rd downs and 0.6 for 1st downs. But looking at the Offensive Drives by Drive annotated with Downs below, we can see that there were 5 first downs, 3 second downs, and only 1 third down. So I looked back at my charts with this in mind and only looked at the drives with a SR of 1.0. The table below has only has four teams because I haven't had time to write code to collect this data and I had to eyeball it. But I think it is a pretty good represenataive sample of our games:
